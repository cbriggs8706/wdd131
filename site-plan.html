Overview

Purpose

This site will serve as a personal portfolio introducing who I am in a creative and interactive way. It includes sections about my hobbies, a personality quiz, a timeline of my life and projects, and a "build your day" scheduler to make the content feel personalized and dynamic.

Audience

The intended audience includes classmates, instructors, future employers, family, and anyone else interested in getting to know me personally or professionally.

Dynamic elements

- Random fun facts that update on click
- Hobby filter using array methods and DOM interaction
- A developer personality quiz using conditional logic and dynamic styling
- A timeline with year and category filters, featuring my personal milestones and web projects
- A day planner that lets the user build a fun schedule with dynamic content
